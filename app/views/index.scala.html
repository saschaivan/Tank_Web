Tank is a artillery strategy game where two players battle against each other on a 2D landscape map.
Each player has their own tank with 100 life points. Upon hitting the target the player loses 10 life points. You win if the other player has 0 life points left.

The idea for the project came because there is another popular game called Shellshock live.
This is much more complicated because there are many different weapons and items you can collect,
but we wanted to implement our own version of this game. In our version there are only 2 Maps,
no items to collect, no different weapons to choose from, but you can drive, shoot and enjoy the good music while playing.
